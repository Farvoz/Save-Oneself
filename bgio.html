Card Placement Game

Rules:

  • Initially, there are 0 cards in the game.
  • First card can be placed anywhere on the board.
  • Each subsequent card must be placed adjacent to an existing card (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  • The playing field is a 4x4 grid, so a maximum of 16 cards can be placed.
  • Win Conditions:
    • Place all 16 cards to win!
    • OR create a diagonal line of 4 cards from any corner to win instantly!
